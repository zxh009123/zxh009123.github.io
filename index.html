I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the University of Vermont advised by Dr. Safwan Wshah. My current research interest lies at the border of computer vision and remote sensing (e.g. visual geo-localization and segmentation/detection in aerial images). I am also interested in image synthesis and 3D reconstruction. Before joining the University of Vermont, I received my M.Sc. degree at the University of California, Santa Cruz in 2020, and I obtained my B.Sc. degree at Michigan State University in 2017.
